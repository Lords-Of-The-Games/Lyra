# Opening Cutscene — Full Dialogue

A quiet forest bench. Light snow drifts in the air.
LYRA sits wearing a tiger-fur cloak.
The OLD MOUSE approaches and sits beside her.

---

## Segment 1 — Small talk / cold weather

OLD MOUSE: It's getting cold out here.  
LYRA: Pretty cold.  
    (She says it a little sharply, annoyed at the small talk.
     He doesn’t notice the tone, fading slightly in thought.)

---

## Segment 2 — Tiger-fur cloak

OLD MOUSE: That's quite a cloak you've got there. Tiger fur, isn't it?  
LYRA: Yeah.  
OLD MOUSE: I've heard stories… someone brought down a big tiger up north.
           Was that you?  
LYRA: I… wasn't alone. Things like that… don't happen alone.  
    (He nods slowly, studying her quietly.)

---

## Segment 3 — Loss

OLD MOUSE: You… lost your home, didn't you?  
LYRA: Yeah… everyone's gone.  
OLD MOUSE: So you're alone now?  
LYRA: Almost… just trying to stay close to someone who isn't doing so well.  
    (She says it quietly, a little weary. He listens silently.)

---

## Segment 4 — Snowfall / Reveal

    (A short pause. Snow begins to fall gently.)

OLD MOUSE: Hey… look at that. It's starting to snow.  
LYRA: Yeah… it's getting colder. We need to go, dad.

    (The OLD MOUSE freezes. Recognition flickers in his eyes.
     Snow thickens, filling the screen with white.)
